With OpenCDN.me you can have them all in one place. That's neat right?

Why should I want to use it?

Being on your own, YOU must be sure that the script you are using is always up to date and you have the latest version available. With us, you don't have to bother. OpenCDN.me will take care of that.


Can't find the script?

Just use the feedback & ideas button and submit your request. It should be added to the OpenCDN.me database in max. 48h


How to use our repository?

For example you want to use jQuery on your site.
Then just paste this code into your HEAD section
<script src="//opencdn.me/script/jquery.js"></script>
